    🇦🇺 Common Scams in Australia

    • Rental Scams: Fake property listings, advance payment requests
    • Job Scams: Fake job offers requiring upfront fees
    • Visa Scams: Fake immigration agents
    • Bank Scams: Fake ATO or bank calls

    📞 Australian Reporting Contacts

    • Scamwatch: scamwatch.gov.au
    • ReportCyber: cyber.gov.au
    • Police: 000 (emergency) or 131 444
    • ACCC: 1300 302 502

    🏦 Bank Fraud Hotlines

    • Commonwealth Bank: 13 2221
    • Westpac: 132 032
    • ANZ: 13 33 50
    • NAB: 13 22 65

    🎓 University Support

    Contact your university's international student office for help with:

    • Accommodation scams
    • Fake tuition payment requests
    • Visa-related fraud
